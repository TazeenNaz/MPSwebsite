Services

Installation Services

Once the planning has been completed, we at Millennium Pension Solutions provide a full range of installation services, including:

  • Assistance in the preparation of the plan documents and Trust.
  • Preparation of the Summary Plan Description.
  • Consultation with attorneys, accountants and other adviser to secure qualifications of the plan from the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
  • Preparation of documents for submission to the Internal Revenue Services, the department of labor and Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC).
  • Establishment of participant and plan file records.
  • Employees Benefit Meetings, if desired.
